CPCCompoundCalendarUnit

internal protocol CPCCompoundCalendarUnit : CPCCalendarUnit, RandomAccessCollection where Self.Element : CPCCalendarUnit, Self.Indices == CountableRange<Int>

Protocol, implementing a collection of smaller units that are contained in this calendar unit.
